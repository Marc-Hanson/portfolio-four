Hand crafted Spirits distilled in the heart of Bewdley.

We are a small craft distillery based on the banks of the River Severn in the heart of England. Nestled away in the historic Georgian town of Bewdley, Worcestershire.

We are located at the award winning Bewdley Brewery and all of the spirits we produce are made from beers produced onsite at the Brewery.

They are distilled and double-filtered to create a smooth clean finish. Each bottle is filled by hand, wax dipped and carefully inspected to ensure the highest quality product every time.
