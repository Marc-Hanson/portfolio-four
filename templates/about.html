About Us

Bewdley Spirits is a small craft distillery based on the banks of the River Severn, in the heart of England in the historic Georgian town of Bewdley, Worcestershire.

Lets tell you a little more about us...

We are located at the award winning Bewdley Brewery and all spirits produced are made from beers produced onsite at the Brewery. All spirits are distilled and double-filtered to create a smooth clean finish. Each bottle is filled by hand and wax dipped to ensure quality every time.

Richard & Sarah Hanson started the business in 2023 from their home in Bewdley, after meeting at school and being married for 30 years they have a very strong foundation.
